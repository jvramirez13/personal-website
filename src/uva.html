About UREG

UREG (Office of the University Registrar) provides academic record services to students, faculty, staff, alumni, and other constituents. Our website is a guide to the many services available to faculty, staff, and students. If you have any questions that are not answered here, please contact TeamUREG at ureg@virginia.edu.

Our Mission

Our purpose is to ensure the accuracy, integrity, and security of the academic records of the University. We provide academic record services to students, faculty, staff, alumni, and other constituents in support of the purpose and goals of the University, through proactive outreach and communication.
